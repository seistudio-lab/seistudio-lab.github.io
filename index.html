Kangwon National University

We develop seismic processing, imaging, sensing, and monitoring technologies to understand complex subsurface systems from land to ocean.

Research Focus

  • Seismic processing and imaging
  • Distributed acoustic sensing
  • Passive seismic and surface waves
  • Survey design and optimization

Research Areas

SeiStudio develops computational and observational methods for imaging, monitoring, and understanding the subsurface.

01

Seismic Processing & Imaging

Denoising, reconstruction, deblending, full-waveform inversion, and reverse-time migration.

02

Distributed Acoustic Sensing

Fiber-optic sensing for submarine cables, coastal monitoring, and seismic applications.

03

Passive Seismic

Interferometry, surface-wave analysis, and subsurface characterization using ambient noise.

04

Survey Optimization

Data-driven and AI-assisted acquisition design for efficient geophysical exploration.
