Kangwon National University

We develop seismic processing, imaging, sensing, and monitoring technologies for understanding complex subsurface and Earth systems—from active faults and engineered sites to submarine cables and polar environments.

Research Focus

  • Structural imaging and active tectonics
  • Submarine fiber-optic sensing
  • Borehole geophysics and geo-energy
  • Polar and extreme Earth systems
  • Inverse problems and data-driven processing

What we do

Research Areas

SeiStudio combines computational geophysics, field observations, and distributed sensing to image and monitor the subsurface.

01

Structural Imaging & Active Tectonics

Reflection imaging and quantitative interpretation of faults and upper-crustal structures.

02

Submarine Fiber-Optic Sensing

DAS and related technologies for coastal, offshore, and infrastructure monitoring.

03

Borehole Geophysics & Geo-Energy

Passive monitoring and site characterization for engineered subsurface systems.

04

Polar & Extreme Earth Systems

Seismic imaging of subglacial lakes, ice–bed systems, and submarine mud volcanoes.

05

Inverse Problems & Data-Driven Processing

Denoising, deblending, deghosting, FWI, compressive sensing, and AI-assisted workflows.
