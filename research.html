Research

Our research connects seismic data processing, numerical imaging, fiber-optic sensing, and field observations.

Seismic Processing & Imaging

Advanced methods for denoising, reconstruction, deblending, velocity estimation, migration, and quantitative interpretation.

Distributed Acoustic Sensing

Repurposing telecommunication and power-cable infrastructure as dense arrays for environmental and geophysical monitoring.

Passive Seismic & Surface Waves

Ambient-noise interferometry, passive MASW, and surface-wave analysis for near-surface and crustal-scale characterization.

Survey Design & Optimization

Compressed sensing, AI-assisted acquisition, and efficient survey geometry design under practical field constraints.

Faults & Geohazards

High-resolution reflection imaging and structural analysis of active and potentially active fault systems.

Energy & Environmental Applications

Applications to offshore resources, CCUS monitoring, deep geological repositories, and coastal infrastructure.
