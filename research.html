Research

Imaging and Sensing Complex Earth Systems

Our research integrates seismic processing, inverse problems, field geophysics, and distributed fiber-optic sensing across terrestrial, marine, engineered, and polar environments.

01

Structural Imaging & Active Tectonics

We develop and apply reflection-seismic processing, migration, and quantitative attributes to resolve upper-crustal structures, active fault zones, and disrupted reflection patterns associated with geohazards.

Seismic reflection imaging Fault-zone characterization CRS stacking Structural attributes
02

Submarine Fiber-Optic Sensing & Marine Geophysics

We investigate distributed acoustic and temperature sensing with submarine fiber-optic cables for coastal-ocean monitoring, offshore structure imaging, ship and wave signal analysis, noise suppression, and infrastructure-scale observations.

Distributed acoustic sensing Submarine cables Wave and vessel signals Marine seismic processing
03

Borehole Geophysics & Geo-Energy Sustainability

Our work addresses seismic-while-drilling, passive seismic monitoring, and integrated geophysical characterization for engineered subsurface systems, including high-level radioactive-waste disposal sites and geo-energy applications.

Borehole geophysics Passive monitoring Deep geological repositories Site characterization
04

Polar & Extreme Earth Systems

We use high-resolution seismic imaging and signal analysis to investigate environments that are difficult to access directly, including Antarctic subglacial lakes and ice–bed systems, Arctic submarine mud volcanoes, and deep-ocean settings.

Antarctic geophysics Subglacial lakes Arctic mud volcanoes Extreme environments
05

Seismic Inverse Problems & Data-Driven Processing

We develop robust methods for denoising, deblending, deghosting, compressive sensing, full-waveform inversion, and AI-assisted acquisition and processing, with an emphasis on practical performance and reproducible workflows.

Denoising and deblending Deghosting Full-waveform inversion Compressive sensing AI-assisted processing
